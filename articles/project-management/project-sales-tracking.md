## <a name="labor-revenue-tracking-view"></a><span data-ttu-id="dd096-108">Arbeitsumsatzverfolgungsansicht</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dd096-108">Labor revenue tracking view</span></span>
