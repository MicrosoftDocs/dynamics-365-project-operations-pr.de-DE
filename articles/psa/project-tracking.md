## <a name="effort-tracking-view"></a><span data-ttu-id="0c0b2-107">Aufwandsnachverfolgungsansicht</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c0b2-107">Effort tracking view</span></span>

## <a name="reprojecting-effort"></a><span data-ttu-id="0c0b2-122">Erneute Hochrechnung des Aufwands</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c0b2-122">Reprojecting effort</span></span>

### <a name="cost-tracking-view"></a><span data-ttu-id="0c0b2-140">Die Ansicht „Kostennachverfolgung“</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c0b2-140">Cost tracking view</span></span>
